# THE AMERICAN ECONOMIC REVIEW

## Vol. 115, No. 10, October 2025

### Treatment Effects in Market Equilibrium
市场均衡中的处理效应

Policy-relevant treatment effect estimation in a marketplace setting requires assessing both the direct treatment benefit and spillovers induced by changes to the market equilibrium. We show how to identify and estimate policy-relevant treatment effects using a unit-randomized trial run within a single large market. A Bernoulli-randomized trial allows consistent estimation of direct effects and of treatment-heterogeneity measures that enable welfare-improving targeting. Estimating spillovers—and providing confidence intervals for the direct effect—requires estimates of price elasticities, which we provide using an augmented experimental design. We illustrate our results using a simulation calibrated to a conditional cash-transfer experiment in the Philippines.

在市场环境中，要估计与政策相关的处理效应，需同时评估直接处理收益以及由市场均衡变化所引发的溢出效应。我们展示了如何通过在单一大型市场内开展的单位随机试验，识别并估计与政策相关的处理效应。伯努利随机试验可对直接效应进行一致估计，并提供用于改善福利目标的处理异质性度量。为估计溢出效应——并为直接效应提供置信区间——需估计价格弹性；我们借助增强实验设计给出这些估计。最后，我们通过一个按菲律宾有条件现金转移实验校准的模拟，演示了我们的结果。

### Consistent Evidence on Duration Dependence of Price Changes
价格变化持续时间依赖的一致证据

We develop a linear GMM estimator of the discrete-time mixed proportional hazard (MPH) model of duration with an arbitrary distribution of unobserved heterogeneity. We allow for competing risks, observable characteristics, and censoring. We prove our estimator is consistent and apply it to the duration of price spells. We find substantial unobserved heterogeneity with economically meaningful implications for the response of output to a monetary policy shock in a model with time-dependent pricing rules and for the degree of state dependence in a model of price plans.

我们构建了一个离散时间混合比例风险（MPH）模型的线性GMM估计量，允许存在任意分布的未观测异质性、竞争风险、可观测特征以及数据删失。我们证明了该估计量具有一致性，并将其应用于价格持续期的分析。研究发现，未观测异质性显著，且在经济意义上对以下两个方面具有重要影响：一是在具有时间依赖定价规则的模型中，产出对货币政策冲击的反应；二是在价格计划模型中状态依赖的程度。

### Politics at Work
职场中的政治倾向

We study how individual political views shape firm behavior and labor market outcomes using new microdata from Brazil. We first show that business owners are considerably more likely to employ copartisan workers. This phenomenon is in part driven by the overlapping of political and social networks. Multiple tests—surveys, event studies, analyses of wage premia and promotions within the firm, and a field experiment—further highlight how business owners' political preferences directly influence firms' employment decisions. A channel of political discrimination appears more relevant than one of political quid pro quo between firms and politicians.

我们利用来自巴西的全新微观数据，研究个人政治观点如何影响企业行为与劳动力市场结果。首先，我们发现企业主明显更倾向于雇佣与其同党派的员工。这一现象部分源于政治网络与社会网络的交织。通过多种检验——包括问卷调查、事件研究、企业内部工资溢价与晋升分析，以及一项实地实验——我们进一步揭示，企业主的政治偏好会直接影响企业的雇佣决策。相比“企业与政客之间政治交易”这一渠道，“政治歧视”似乎更能解释上述现象。

### Place-Based Redistribution
基于地点的再分配

We study optimal income taxation in a spatial equilibrium model with heterogeneous locational preferences, labor supply decisions, and competitive housing and labor markets. Expressions characterizing the optimal tax schedule in each community are provided that capture the fiscal externalities associated with migration and the effects of redistribution between households and landlords. Correlation between skill and locational preferences yields optimal transfers to poor areas, while sorting based on comparative advantage can motivate transfers in either direction. A calibration to areas targeted by the US Empowerment Zone program yields sizable optimal spatial transfers that are sensitive to assumed levels of migration responsiveness.

我们研究了一个包含异质性区位偏好、劳动供给决策以及竞争性住房与劳动力市场的空间均衡模型中的最优所得税问题。给出了刻画每个社区最优税收安排的表达式，这些表达式捕捉了与迁移相关的财政外部性以及再分配对家庭与房东之间关系的影响。当技能与区位偏好存在相关性时，会导致对贫困地区的最优转移支付；而基于比较优势的排序则可能促使转移支付朝任一方向进行。通过对美国“赋权区”（Empowerment Zone）项目所针对地区进行的校准，我们发现最优的空间转移支付规模可观，且对迁移响应程度的假设非常敏感。

### Monetary Policy and the Labor Market: A Quasi-experiment in Sweden
货币政策与劳动力市场：来自瑞典的一项准实验

We analyze a monetary quasi-experiment in Sweden from 2010–2011, when the Riksbank raised the interest rate substantially. We argue that this increase was beyond what labor market conditions warranted, driven instead by new concerns about financial stability. Using a battery of specifications that rule out domestic or international confounders, we show that this monetary tightening led to a substantial economic contraction, raising unemployment by 1–2 percentage points. Using administrative microdata, we find that sectors with nominal wage rigidity drove much of the response and that the monetary contraction was more regressive than the typical business cycle.

我们分析了瑞典在2010–2011年间的一项货币准实验，当时瑞典央行大幅上调了利率。我们认为，这一加息幅度超出了劳动力市场状况所对应的合理水平，主要是出于对金融稳定的新担忧所驱动。通过一系列排除国内外混杂因素的设定，我们发现这一货币紧缩政策导致了显著的经济收缩，使失业率上升了1–2个百分点。借助行政微观数据，我们发现名义工资刚性较强的行业是反应的主要来源，且此次货币紧缩的回归性（对低收入群体冲击更大）比典型的商业周期更为显著。

### Apart but Connected: Online Tutoring, Cognitive Outcomes, and Soft Skills
分离却相连：在线辅导、认知结果与非认知能力

We study the Tutoring Online Program (TOP), where tutoring is entirely online and tutors are volunteer university students matched with underprivileged middle school students. We leverage random assignment to estimate effects during and after the pandemic (2020 and 2022), investigating channels of impact. Three hours of individual tutoring per week increased math performance by 0.22 SD in 2020 and 0.20 SD in 2022. Higher dosage yielded stronger effects, while group tutoring smaller effects. TOP enhanced students' aspirations, socioemotional skills, and psychological well-being, but only during school closures. We also estimate the impact of TOP on tutors, finding an increase in empathy.

我们研究了“在线辅导项目”（TOP），该项目完全在线进行，由大学生志愿者担任辅导员，与弱势初中生配对。利用随机分配，我们估算了2020年（疫情期间）和2022年（疫情后）的效果，并探讨了影响机制。每周三小时的一对一辅导使数学成绩在2020年提高了0.22个标准差，2022年提高了0.20个标准差。辅导时间越长，效果越强；而小组辅导的效果较小。TOP还提升了学生的抱负、社会情感能力和心理健康，但仅在学校停课期间显著。我们还估算了TOP对辅导员的影响，发现他们的同理心有所增强。

### The Value of Software
软件的价值

Software is one of the most important assets that needs to be priced in the digital economy. It has emerged as a disruptive technology, with companies primarily valued for their software offerings growing from 2 percent to 13 percent of market share between 1996 and 2023. We document persistent anomalies in growth forecasts and stock returns for software companies, indicating significant deviations from rational expectations over multiple decades. Our findings are consistent with Bayesian investors gradually learning about software's growing importance, highlighting how markets can be very slow to discern fundamental shifts from transient shocks in noisy data.

软件是数字经济中亟需定价的最重要资产之一。它已成为一种颠覆性技术：1996—2023 年间，因软件产品而受市场估值的公司，其市场份额从 2% 跃升至 13%。我们记录了软件公司在增长预测与股票回报上长期存在的异常现象，显示其显著且持续多年地偏离理性预期。结果与贝叶斯投资者逐步学习软件重要性提升的过程相吻合，凸显市场在嘈杂数据中区分根本性转变与短暂冲击时可能极其迟缓。

### Do Credit Conditions Move House Prices?
信贷状况会推动房价涨跌吗？

Did credit drive the 2000s housing cycle? The existing literature's findings range from credit having no effect to credit explaining most of the cycle. We show that these disparate results hinge on the extent to which landlords absorb credit-driven demand, which depends on the degree of housing market segmentation. We develop a model that nests cases between the extremes of no segmentation and perfect segmentation typically considered, estimate an elasticity that pins down the degree of segmentation, and use it to calibrate our model. We find credit standards played an important role, explaining 32 percent to 53 percent of the boom.

2000 年代的房地产周期是信贷驱动的吗？现有文献的结论大相径庭：从“信贷毫无影响”到“信贷解释绝大部分波动”皆有。我们指出，这些分歧关键取决于房东在多大程度上吸纳了信贷引致的需求，而这又由住房市场的分割程度决定。我们构建了一个模型，把以往文献通常只考虑的“无分割”与“完全分割”两种极端情形纳入统一框架；估计出一个刻画分割程度的弹性参数，并据此校准模型。结果显示，信贷标准确实发挥了重要作用，可以解释 32%–53% 的房价繁荣。

### Diffusion of Reproductive Health Behavior through International Migration: Effects on Origin-Country Fertility
国际移民对生殖健康行为的扩散：对原籍国生育率的影响

International migrants may facilitate the transmission of ideas across countries. We examine the impact of migrant exposure to reproductive health policies on origin-country fertility in the Philippines. We exploit temporal variation in destination-country reproductive health policies combined with spatial variation across Philippine provinces in their migration intensity and historical composition of migrant destinations. Migrant exposure to more liberalized reproductive health policies reduces origin-community fertility. This reduction is driven by increased adoption of modern contraceptives. Visible policy changes, such as commercial advertising of contraceptives, lead to this change in behavior. Firmly established family planning values moderate the fertility response.

国际移民可能促进观念在国家间的传播。本文考察了菲律宾移民接触目的地国生殖健康政策对其原籍国生育率的影响。我们利用目的地国生殖健康政策在时间维度上的变化，结合菲律宾各省在移民强度及历史移民目的地构成方面的空间差异，进行识别分析。研究发现，当移民接触到更为宽松的生殖健康政策时，其原籍社区的生育率会下降。这一下降主要源于现代避孕措施使用率的提高。诸如避孕药具商业广告等显性的政策变化，推动了此类行为的转变。而那些原本已牢固确立计划生育价值观的社区，则对生育率变化表现出较弱的反应。

### Intermediated Asymmetric Information, Compensation, and Career Prospects
中介不对称信息、薪酬与职业前景

Adverse selection benefits firms able to identify talent. An informed intermediary expropriates agents' ability by threatening to fire and expose them to undervaluation of their skill. An agent's track record gradually reduces intermediary's information advantage. In response, the intermediary starts churning well-performing agents she knows are less skilled. The accelerated reduction in information advantage boosts profits, as retained agents accept below-reservation wages to build reputation faster. Agents prefer starting their careers working for an intermediary, as benefits from building reputation faster more than offset expropriation costs. Our analysis applies to professions where talent is essential and performance is publicly observable.

逆向选择反而让能识别才能的企业受益。知情中介会“榨取”代理人的能力：以解雇并曝光其真实（被低估）技能相威胁，迫使代理人接受低薪。随着代理人的业绩记录不断积累，中介的信息优势逐渐削弱；为应对这一局面，中介开始频繁淘汰那些她明知“表现好但实力一般”的代理人。信息优势的加速丧失反而提高了利润——因为留下来的代理人为了更快建立声誉，甘愿接受低于保留工资的合同。代理人仍愿意先在中介麾下起步：快速累积声誉带来的长期收益，足以抵消被“榨取”的短期成本。我们的分析适用于那些“天赋至关重要且业绩可公开观测”的职业。
